Project Title: ExitSense – Smart Reminders Before You Leave

Brief Explanation of the Proposed Implementation:
ExitSense is an Android application developed using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose that reminds users about important items before they leave a location such as home, hostel, college, or library. The user can save items like ID card, charger, lab file, wallet, and water bottle along with the days, time, and location where they are needed. The application uses location services, WorkManager, notifications, and reminder scheduling to detect when the user is leaving a saved place and then displays a smart reminder. The app will include features such as navigation drawer, tabs, history tracking, custom UI components, runtime permissions, DataStore settings, and notification channels. It will also maintain a history of forgotten items and suggest reminders based on the user’s habits.
